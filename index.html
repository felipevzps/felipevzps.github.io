I'm a M.Sc. student in bioinformatics at the Center for Nuclear Energy in Agriculture - University of São Paulo, working in the LabBCES.

My research focuses on understanding the variability of ncRNAs in sugarcane and their functional roles.

Before grad school, I was an undergraduate student in biotechnology at the University of São Carlos. I joined LabBCES in 2019, where I fell in love with bioinformatics <3.
